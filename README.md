PC Store Management System

A PC Store Management System designed to streamline the operations of a computer retail store. This project helps manage inventory, sales, customers, and billing efficiently through a structured and user-friendly interface.

Features
Manage PC components and products (CPU, GPU, RAM, etc.)
Inventory management with stock tracking
Billing and invoice generation
Customer management system
Search and filter products
Sales tracking and records
